Smart Note Privacy Policy

Smart Note respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled within our application.

1. Local Data Storage All notes and personal content are stored locally on your device. The app does not automatically upload or share any user data with external servers.

2. No Advertising or Analytics Smart Note does not include any advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking tools. Your data is never used for profiling or advertising purposes.

3. Optional AI Summarization Feature If you choose to use the AI assistant, your selected text is securely sent to a third-party AI provider (such as OpenAI or Google Gemini) solely for the purpose of generating a summary. This feature is fully optional and only activated when you request it.

4. Use of Third-Party APIs The app may use APIs provided by external services (e.g., OpenAI, Google). We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand how they process data.

5. Your Control You can use all Smart Note features without ever using the AI assistant. No data is collected or sent externally unless you explicitly opt in.

6. Data Collection We do not collect or store personal data. No user-identifiable information is transmitted from your device unless you explicitly choose to do so.

By using Smart Note, you are assured that your privacy is respected and your data remains under your control.
